Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish that has become a staple in many households around the world. It is a layered pasta dish that typically consists of wide noodles, tomato sauce, cheese, and meat. The noodles are layered with the tomato sauce, cheese, and meat, then baked until the cheese is melted and bubbly. While the classic recipe includes ground beef or pork, there are many variations that use chicken, turkey, or even vegetables for a vegetarian option. Some recipes also include ricotta cheese, spinach, or mushrooms for added flavor and nutrition. Lasagna is a hearty and satisfying meal that is perfect for a cozy dinner with family and friends.

Making lasagna from scratch can be a bit time-consuming, but it is well worth the effort. The first step is to prepare the tomato sauce, which usually involves saut√©ing onions and garlic in olive oil, then adding canned tomatoes, herbs, and spices. The meat is then cooked and seasoned before being added to the sauce. The next step is to boil the noodles until al dente, then layer them in a baking dish with the sauce and cheese. The process is repeated until the dish is full, then it is baked in the oven for 30-40 minutes until the cheese is melted and golden brown. The end result is a delicious and comforting meal that is sure to satisfy even the pickiest of eaters.

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Meat
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Herbs and spices

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  2. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add lasagna noodles and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet over medium heat, cook the ground beef, onion, and garlic until browned. Add in the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, tomato sauce, and water. Season with sugar, basil, fennel seeds, Italian seasoning, 1 tablespoon salt, pepper, and 2 tablespoons parsley. Simmer for about 1 hour, stirring occasionally.
  4. In a mixing bowl, combine ricotta cheese with egg, remaining parsley, and 1/2 teaspoon salt.
  5. To assemble, spread 1 1/2 cups of meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish. Arrange 6 noodles lengthwise over meat sauce. Spread with one half of the ricotta cheese mixture. Top with a third of mozzarella cheese slices. Spoon 1 1/2 cups meat sauce over mozzarella, and sprinkle with 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese. Repeat layers, and top with remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
  6. Cover with foil and bake in preheated oven for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake an additional 25 minutes. Cool for 15 minutes before serving.
